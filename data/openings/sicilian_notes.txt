Sicilian Defence Training Notes

Against 1.e4 c5, Black fights for the center indirectly. Key ideas:
- Use the c-file after exchanges.
- Develop pieces quickly.
- Do not push too many pawns before king safety.
- In e6 and a6 structures, Black often prepares ...b5 and queenside expansion.

Training focus:
Ask the player to explain why ...a6 and ...b5 are useful before memorizing moves.
