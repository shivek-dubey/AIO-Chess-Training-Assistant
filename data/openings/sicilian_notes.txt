Sicilian Dragon Opening

Moves:
1.e4 c5
2.Nf3 d6
3.d4 cxd4
4.Nxd4 Nf6
5.Nc3 g6

Main Ideas for Black:
- Develop the bishop to g7.
- Castle kingside quickly.
- Apply pressure on the long diagonal a1-h8.
- Counterattack on the queenside using Rc8 and Qa5.

Main Ideas for White:
- Develop pieces rapidly.
- Consider Yugoslav Attack with Be3, f3, Qd2 and long castling.
- Launch kingside pawn storm using h4 and h5.

Typical Plans:
Black:
- Rc8
- Ne5
- Qa5
- Rfc8

White:
- Bh6 exchange
- h4-h5 pawn storm
- Sacrifices on h5 and e6

Common Tactical Themes:
- Exchange sacrifice on c3.
- Bishop sacrifice on h3.
- Knight jump to e5.
