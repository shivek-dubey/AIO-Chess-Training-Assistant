King and Pawn Endgame: Opposition

Opposition means placing your king in front of the opponent king with one square between them, so the opponent must move away.

Direct opposition: kings are close with one square between them.
Distant opposition: kings are far apart but aligned on the same file, rank, or diagonal.

Simple rule:
If your king can force the enemy king to move away, your pawn may promote.

Training task:
Set up king and pawn positions and ask the player to find whether the position is win or draw.
