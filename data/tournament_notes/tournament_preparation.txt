Tournament Preparation Checklist

Before the game:
- Sleep well.
- Eat light food.
- Review only familiar openings.
- Solve 5 easy tactics, not very hard puzzles.
- Stay calm in the first 10 moves.

During the game:
- Check opponent threats.
- Do not move instantly.
- In winning positions, reduce counterplay.
- In worse positions, create practical problems.

After the game:
- Write one good move, one mistake, and one lesson.
